Date of Birth Field Test

Test Instructions:

  • Type numbers in the DOB field - it will auto-format as dd-mm-yyyy
  • Try typing: 15031990 → will become 15-03-1990
  • Invalid dates will show errors immediately
  • Must be 18+ years old to register
  • Cannot be a future date

Valid Date Examples:

15-03-1990 (Valid, 18+ years old)
01-01-1985 (Valid, 18+ years old)
31-12-2000 (Valid, 18+ years old)

Invalid Date Examples:

32-13-2024 (Invalid day/month)
01-01-2030 (Future date)
01-01-2010 (Under 18 years old)
15/03/1990 (Wrong format)
